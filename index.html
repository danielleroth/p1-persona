About

Hello there, friend! I'm Danielle Roth. This is my personal website, where I share some of my design and creative works. I am currently a student at Eastern Washington University in the Bachelor of Fine Arts of Visual Communication Design program. I was born and raised in Spokane, Washington, and love the Inland Northwest! I am happily married to my awesome husband (David) for 2 years, and we live near Cheney where I attend school. I love reading, playing music, backpacking, horseback riding, designing, crocheting, and spending time with friends and family. A favorite movie series of David's and mine is Star Wars--feeling nerdy? Use the force, Luke (yes, we even read the books). One of my favorite contemporary music artists is Josh Garrels; if you've never heard him before, take a listen here.

If you'd like to follow me on Twitter, my username is belovedhos2-23, or you can like my page on Facebook. Thanks for stopping by!
